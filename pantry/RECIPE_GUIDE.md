## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Recipe Creation
1. **Take photos in good lighting** - makes browsing recipes more enjoyable
2. **Be specific with ingredient names** - helps with pantry matching
3. **Use standard units** - cup, tbsp, tsp, oz, lb, g, etc.
4. **Add prep notes** - "diced", "room temperature", "beaten"
5. **Set timers on steps** - makes cooking mode super helpful

### Ingredient Matching
- App uses fuzzy matching: "chicken breast" matches "chicken"
- Handles plural forms and variations
- Shows substitution suggestions for missing items

### Cooking Mode Tips
- **Keep iPad on counter** - perfect hands-free cooking station
- **Large buttons** - easy to tap with messy hands
- **Screen won't sleep** - stays awake during cooking
- **Use timers** - no need to set separate kitchen timers

### Organization
1. **Favorite frequently-used recipes** - easy to filter
2. **Use descriptive names** - easier to search
3. **Rate after cooking** - helps remember favorites
4. **Add personal notes** - modifications, tips, substitutions
5. **Duplicate before modifying** - keep original and create variations
